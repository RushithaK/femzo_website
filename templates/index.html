What is "Femzo"?

Femzo is a web application which we have developed to provide a platform for women/girls who get threating calls and face physicals and mental abuses in day-to-day life and social media.

Why should you use "Femzo"?

With the help of this website victims or their companions can (FAC) file an instant complaint by registering and uploading necessary documents, an immediate rescue team will be appointed. She can contact or approach the nearby police stations through our website.

Pros of "Femzo"

i) File an instant complaint & get an immediate response.
ii) Learn self-defence & rescue yourselves from unsafe situations.
iii) Get police station details.

How to Use "Femzo"

  • To begin with, Register your self by clicking on sign up/sign in menu
  • After registering, login into your account with valid credentials.
  • Once, you have logged in, navigate yourself to "File a Complaint" menu.
  • Fill in all the details as required. Once all the required info is filled. Click on "Submit" button.
  • After submitting the form you will get an confirmation on your registered E-mail and Phone number.
  • ASAP, a police/she team will assist you.
